Summary

The Shawshank Redemption follows the story of Andy Dufresne, a banker sentenced to life in Shawshank State Prison for a crime he didn't commit. Over decades he forges an unlikely friendship with Red, finds ways to maintain hope, and quietly engineers a plan for freedom that changes lives. It's a tale of resilience, friendship, and redemption.
